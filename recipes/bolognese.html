Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese is one of the few dishes I can cook without needing a recipe. The recipe that I use came from my father's recipe folder. I'm not sure where he copied the recipe from.

Spaghetti bolognese is basically a meat sauce with a tomato base on top of spaghetti. The sauce is slow-cooked for three quarters of an hour. At the end of cooking, the sauce is rich with the taste of the herbs, spices and wine.

Ingredients

  • Mince
  • Canned tomatoes
  • tomato paste
  • onion
  • garlic
  • red wine
  • oregano
  • basil
  • salt
  • pepper
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Brown mince in olive oil.
  2. Remove mince from pan and drain.
  3. Soften chopped garlic and onion in pan in olive oil.
  4. Add brown mince, canned tomato and tomato paste.
  5. Add red wine, herbs and spices.
  6. Bring to heat and then simmer for 45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Serve sauce on top of spaghetti and top with parmesan.
