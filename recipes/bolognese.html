Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

This is one of the few recipes that I know how to cook well. I got the recipe from my parents' cookbook.

Ingredients

  • Mince
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Pepper
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Oregano
  • Basil
  • Red wine

Steps

  1. Cook mince and drain fat
  2. Chop onion and garlic and soften in pan.
  3. Cook mince with onion and garlic
  4. Add tomatoes, tomato paste, herbs and red wine
  5. Bring to boil, then simmer for 45 minutes.
