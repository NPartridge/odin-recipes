Crustless Quiche

Description

This is a very simple one-dish crustless quiche. I learned how to make it at "manual" while I was at primary school. It has since become a family favourite.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Onions
  • Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Cook bacon in microwave for 2 minutes
  2. Chop onion and soften in pan
  3. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl
  4. Melt butter in microwave
  5. Combine milk, eggs, and melted butter
  6. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and mix well
  7. Add bacon and onion to the mixture
  8. Grease a glass dish with butter and pour in quiche mixture
  9. Cook quiche for approximately 20 minutes in oven
