Soft-boiled eggs with toast

Description

A quick and easy meal.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Bread
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Place eggs in cold water and bring to boil. Simmer for 2 - 3 minutes
  2. While eggs are cooking, toast bread
  3. Remove eggs from water and butter toast
  4. Cut toast into strips to dip into egg
