Crustless Quiche

Description

This is a very simple one-pot crustless quiche. I learned how to make it at "manual" while I was at primary school. It has since become a family favourite.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Bacon
  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Onions
  • Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Cook bacon in microwave for 2 minutes.
  2. Chop and soften onion in a pan
  3. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl
  4. Melt butter in microwave
  5. Combine milk, eggs and melted butter
  6. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and mix well
  7. Add bacon and onion to the mixture
  8. Grease a glass dish with butter and pour in quiche mixture
  9. Cook quiche for approximately half an hour in oven.
