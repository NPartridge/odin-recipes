Quiche

Description

I learned this recipe at "manual". "Manual" was an off-school class, held weekly, which was either cooking or wood-working. My family still make this recipe 35 years later!

The quiche is crustless and made with eggs, flour, bacon, onion and cheese.

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • flour
  • baking powder
  • milk
  • cheese
  • cooked bacon
  • onion
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Mix flour, baking powder, salt and pepper in a bowl
  2. beat eggs and mix into dry ingredients
  3. Chop cooked bacon into slices and add to bowl
  4. Chop onion and add to bowl
  5. Grate cheese and add one cup to bowl
  6. Mix ingredients in bowl
  7. Grease a dish and add ingredients
  8. Top dish with remaining grated cheese
  9. Cook quiche for 20 minutes or until brown on top.
